Last updated 2021-08-30

0.1 Effect of format on timing (Block 1 data)

We used a multilevel model, nesting log-seconds within participant to account for dependence. Our primary predictor was format. Here, we use only Block 1 data. Results are depicted in Figure 1. The full distribution of timing (in log-seconds) is shown in Figure 2. Tests of pairwise comparisons are shown in Table 1.

item_block1 = filter(items_df, block == "1")

mod.format_b1 = lmer(seconds_log~format + (1|proid), 
                  data = item_block1)
